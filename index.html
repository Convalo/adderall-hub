WHAT CAN YOU DO?

Look to yourself, as well as to your own medicine cabinet. If you use prescription painkillers, or attention disorder medication such as Adderall, take stock of your supply. Take note of the type of drugs you have, and their amount.

With that baseline, keep track over your medications to see if any of them beyond your own use are missing.

It’s better to question them immediately, and if your kids have stolen your medication for their own uses, try to counsel them on not just the dangers of using the drugs, but the criminality of their actions. No, this isn’t easy pickings here. There are real consequences involved.
